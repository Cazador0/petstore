# **App Name**: Royal Paws Boutique

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog: Browse and search for pet supplies and products.
- Service Listings: Discover and book pet services such as grooming, vet checkups, training, and boarding.
- Pet Profiles: Showcase pets available for purchase or adoption, with detailed profiles.
- AI-Powered Product Recommendations: Suggest relevant products based on pet type, breed, and customer purchase history, acting as a product recommendation tool.
- Customer Accounts: Enable customers to manage their profiles, track orders, and save favorite items.
- Shopping Cart and Checkout: Provide a seamless shopping experience with secure payment options.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep purple (#663399) for elegance and royalty.
- Background color: Dark grey (#222222) to enhance the purple and create a sophisticated look.
- Accent color: Lavender (#E6E6FA) to highlight important elements and provide contrast.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern serif for an elegant, fashionable feel; body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif suitable for longer text.
- Use simple, elegant line icons in white or lavender.
- Clean and minimalist design with plenty of whitespace to create a sense of luxury and sophistication.
- Subtle animations and transitions to enhance the user experience.
